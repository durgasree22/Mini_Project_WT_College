Health Tips & Facts

Healthy Eating

Eating a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables can boost your immune system. Tip: Aim to fill half your plate with fruits and veggies!



Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water supports digestion, energy levels, and skin health. Aim for at least 8 glasses daily.

Get Moving

Regular exercise improves heart health and mood. A 30-minute daily walk has benefits!

Sleep Well

Quality sleep is essential for overall well-being. Aim for 7-9 hours per night for optimal health.

Milestones in Medical History

  • The Discovery of Penicillin

    In 1928, Alexander Fleming’s accidental discovery revolutionized treatment for bacterial infections.

    Milestone: 1928

  • The First Successful Heart Transplant

    Dr. Christiaan Barnard performed the first heart transplant, paving the way for organ transplants.

    Milestone: 1967

  • The Introduction of Vaccines

    Edward Jenner’s work on smallpox in 1796 laid the foundation for modern vaccination methods.

    Milestone: 1796

  • The Introduction of Anesthesia

    In the 19th century, the use of anesthesia transformed surgical practices, making operations less painful.

    Milestone: 1846

  • The Discovery of DNA Structure

    James Watson and Francis Crick revealed the double-helix structure of DNA, leading to advancements in genetics.

    Milestone: 1953
